# Literacy Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview

Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing literacy trends across the United States to identify underserved populations and support data-driven outreach, resource allocation, and strategic planning for literacy-focused nonprofit and community programs.

The analysis uses data from the National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) and connects literacy performance with demographic, geographic, and socioeconomic factors to uncover actionable insights for education and community development initiatives.

## Business Problem

Organizations aiming to improve literacy outcomes often face challenges in identifying where to focus resources and which populations require the most support.

Key questions addressed:

Which regions and counties show the lowest literacy performance?
How does poverty correlate with literacy outcomes?
Which demographic groups are most impacted?
Where should targeted interventions be prioritized?

## Key Objectives
Identify geographic areas with low literacy performance
Analyze relationships between poverty levels and literacy rates
Segment literacy trends across age groups and demographics
Highlight high-priority counties for targeted intervention
Support strategic decision-making for outreach and funding allocation

## Dataset
The dataset is from 2024 National Center for Education Statistics literacy report.

### Literacy Trends & National Benchmarking
Displays decline in reading scores over time
Highlights turning points in literacy performance
Compares county-level literacy against national averages

Business Insight:
Long-term declines signal need for sustained intervention strategies rather than short-term programs.

### State-Level Literacy Distribution
Choropleth map showing literacy scores by state
Visual identification of high- and low-performing regions

Business Insight:
Regional disparities reveal where statewide funding or policy changes may be required.

### Poverty vs Literacy Analysis
Compares literacy scores across poverty ranges
Includes national average benchmark

Business Insight:
Clear inverse relationship between poverty and literacy supports prioritizing high-poverty regions for intervention programs.

### County-Level Intervention Targeting
Identifies top counties for intervention based on priority score

Business Insight:
Enables targeted allocation of resources to maximize impact and improve program ROI.

Business Insight:

## Key Insights
Higher poverty levels strongly correlate with lower literacy performance
Southern and certain western regions show consistently lower literacy scores
Significant variation exists across counties within the same state
Specific demographic groups experience lower literacy outcomes
Targeted, data-driven intervention strategies are more effective than broad approaches
